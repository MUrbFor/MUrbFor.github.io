THIS WILL BE THE MIAN TITLE

This framework is designed to break down net zero into smaller themes and actions.

The purpose is to help Innovate UK and local authorities understand the range of things that councils can do to get to net zero – with different levels of focus, from big strategic challenges through to smaller, tactical challenges.

It then gives a snapshot of the solutions that companies are offering, to give an at-a-glance assessment of where private firms fit in to net zero plans.
